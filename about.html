About Me

Fact #1

My name is Justin Serrano and I was born in the Philippines (April 15, 1998). Then, I came to Canada. I moved to Montreal, Quebec at the age of 2 and moved again to Calgary, Alberta at the age of 11.



Fact #2

I like to dance. I perform at events with my dance group and sometimes with friends just for fun. As a kid, I didn't really enjoy dancing because I wasn't really good at it. I was forced to dance as a source of entertainment for family parties and adults would enjoy watching me awkwardly dance. As I got older, I got back to dancing and took part in talent shows which sparked my interest in dancing.
